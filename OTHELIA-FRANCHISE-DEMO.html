Arc Diagram — Character Threads
Every character drawn as a colored arc connecting all the titles they appear in. Arc height shows story distance. See Duncan Idaho's arc span from Dune (1965) through God Emperor — 3,500 years of story time in one view.
  • Character arcs as bezier curves above a story timeline
  • Arc height = story time between appearances
  • Click any character to isolate their thread
  • Color dots on each title show which characters appear
  • Hover arcs for arc summary tooltips
Pure SVG • No dependencies

Force Graph — Network of Connections
D3 physics simulation. Title nodes and character nodes float freely in space. Characters gravitationally pull toward every title they appear in — natural clusters form around shared cast. Drag any node.
  • D3 force simulation — nodes float and cluster naturally
  • Character nodes pull toward their titles
  • Click any node to highlight all connections
  • Three modes: All / Characters Only / Titles Only
  • Zoom and drag the entire graph
